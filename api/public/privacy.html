Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 11, 2026

box fraise ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Rajzyngier Research and comprises the box fraise iOS application and the website at fraise.maison. This policy explains what information we collect across the full platform — including ordering, membership, the social layer, NFC contact exchange, token activity, and the editorial portal — how we use it, and your rights with respect to it.

We collect only what is necessary to operate the platform. We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

Information we collect

When you place an order:

  • Email address — to send your order confirmation and pickup notification.
  • Order details — variety, chocolate, finish, quantity, collection time. Stored to fulfil your order and display your order history.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Push notification token — if you grant permission, we store a device token to notify you when your order is ready. Revocable at any time in iOS Settings.

When you create an account or sign in with Apple:

  • Apple user identifier — a stable, anonymised identifier issued by Apple, used to link your account across sessions. We do not receive your real Apple ID email unless you choose to share it.
  • Display name — if provided during Apple Sign In.

When you engage with the social platform:

  • NFC tap records — each box tap logs the variety tapped, timestamp, and the resulting time credit. This forms your time bank balance and social tier.
  • Social tier — Standard, Reserve, or Estate — derived from your time bank balance. Visible to platform staff and, in aggregate, on the leaderboard.
  • Lifetime credit score — the running total of all time credits ever earned. Used for the platform leaderboard. Never decreases.
  • NFC contact exchanges — when you exchange a 6-character NFC code with another member, both parties' identifiers are logged as a permanent contact record. This record cannot be deleted.

When you use the editorial or portal features:

  • Submitted content — text, photos, and video you submit to the editorial feed or the explicit portal. Content is stored on Cloudinary. You retain ownership; we hold a licence to display it within the platform.
  • Portrait photos — photos assigned to your member profile by platform operators, stored on Cloudinary.

When you hold or trade tokens:

  • Token records — each strawberry token is permanently linked to the variety and harvest it came from, and to the member account that received it. Transfer history is logged and cannot be deleted, as it forms the provenance chain.
How we use your information
  • To process and fulfil your orders.
  • To send transactional emails and push notifications related to your orders and account.
  • To calculate and maintain your time bank balance and social tier.
  • To display your order history, token holdings, and contact log in the app.
  • To operate the platform leaderboard using lifetime credit scores.
  • To moderate platform content and enforce community standards.
  • To issue and track Fraise (FRS) tokens and strawberry variety tokens.

We do not use your information for marketing, behavioural profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the platform.

Third-party services
  • Stripe — payment processing. stripe.com/privacy
  • Resend — transactional email delivery. resend.com/privacy
  • Expo — push notification infrastructure. expo.dev/privacy
  • Railway — server and database hosting. railway.app/legal/privacy
  • Cloudinary — image and video storage for editorial content, portraits, and portal uploads. cloudinary.com/privacy
  • Apple — Sign In with Apple identity services. apple.com/legal/privacy
Permanent records

Certain records within the platform are by design permanent and cannot be deleted:

  • NFC contact exchange logs — once two members exchange codes, the contact record is permanent on both sides.
  • Token provenance chains — transfer history for strawberry tokens is permanently retained as part of the harvest ledger. On account deletion, your identity within the chain is anonymised, but the chain itself is preserved.
Data retention

Order records and account data are retained for business and legal purposes. If you would like your account and associated data deleted, contact us at austin@fraise.maison and we will process the request within 30 days, subject to the permanent record exceptions noted above.

Children

The box fraise app and platform are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app or platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at austin@fraise.maison.
